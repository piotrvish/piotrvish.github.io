About

I am currently a student at Higher Art and Design School in Geneva. I like typography, design, cinematorgraphy and writing. Don't hesitate to drop me a message.
I smile, trying to bring some enthusiasm into my life. But inside, I’m already planning what comes next. The new projects, the people I meet, the sleepless nights when new ideas arrive, the mess, the noise. As usual, nobody sees any of that. And it isn't necessarily a bad or good thing, it's just as it is.
